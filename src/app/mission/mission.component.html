Our Mission



Mwangaza aims to reach a target of 20,000 libraries and is working hard to achieve this goal.

Mwangaza is open to getting partners or volunteers who will join our journey to achieve this great milestone.

We are committed to ensuring that we can be able to reach as many rural schools as possible through this library program.

Mwangaza has been able to achieve one major milestone of completing a library in Kongoini Primary school Murang'a County.
