Why Are Our Projects Important ...



Social Reasons

There is strong evidence that reading for pleasure can increase empathy, improve relationships with others, reduce the symptoms of depression and the risk of dementia, and improve wellbeing throughout life. ... Reading can also help children develop empathy and promote their emotional wellbeing in all sorts of ways

Psychological Reasons

Reading promotes greater maturity, increases discipline and lays the basis for moral literacy. It sparks curiosity about people, places and things and also satisfies the child's curiosity by providing explanations of how things work. It exposes the child to a range of problem-solving techniques.In addition, early reading ignites the child's creativity & imagination.


Education Purposes

Exposure to different viewpoints: One purpose of assigned academic readings is to give students exposure to different viewpoints and ideas. ... Such experience can help you wrestle with ideas and beliefs in new ways and develop a better understanding of how others' views differ from your own.

Ligusitic Reasons

Reading is a complex and purposeful sociocultural, cognitive, and linguistic process in which readers simultaneously use their knowledge of spoken and written language, their knowledge of the topic of the text, and their knowledge of their culture to construct meaning with text.
