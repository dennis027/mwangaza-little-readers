Mwangaza Library Projects









Building Libraries



Mwangaza Little Readers is commited to building Libraries for students for the following reasons:

  • They offer free educational resources to everyone.
  • They play an important role in languages learning enhancement.
  • Libraries make communities healthier through young education.
  • They preserve history, and more importantly, truth.
  • Libraries help connect communities.




Enhancing Libraries






Mwangaza Little Readers enhance already made Libraries but have inadequates resources to enable them meet ther required stardards through:

  • Enhancing and Repairing Infrastructures
  • Buying Books and Stories for the Library
  • Employing staffs who will help in Library running
  • Bringing new Infrastructure in the library


Motivating Students








Mwangaza Little Readers through the volunteers and partners help in motivation and naturing of young minds for the following aims

  • Motivation fosters creativity and critical thinking
  • Motivation cultivates resilience and self-assurance
  • Encourage self-efficacy
  • Normalise the struggle
  • Develop optimally challenging, mastery-oriented goals
